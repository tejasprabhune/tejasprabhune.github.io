Tejas Prabhune

I am a second-year EECS major at UC Berkeley, advised by Dr. Gopala K. Anumanchipalli, Peter Wu, Cheol Jun Cho, and Jiachen Lian. I primarily work at the Berkeley Speech Group within Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research (BAIR).

My research is focused on visualizing the vocal tract using speech-driven 3D avatars, brain-to-computer interfaces (BCI) for virtual embodiment, and generating realistic audio-visual dialogues using avatar-based LLMs.
